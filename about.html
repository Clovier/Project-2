BACKGROUND

My passion for vinyl started when I was young, sitting next to my grandma while she played me some of her favorite records - Bennie and the Jetsby Elton John, or Can't Help Falling in Love by Elvis Presley. She would always tell me about where each of the record came from, and over time, some of these songs became part of my childhood.

MY STORY

When my grandma passed her vinyl collection down to me, I wanted to carry on what she started.

Today, that passion turned into a journey. I find myself searching for records in small shops and side streets around the world, each one holding a story of where I've been.
